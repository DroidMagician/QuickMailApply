# Privacy Policy — QuickMail Apply

**Last updated:** June 19, 2026

QuickMail Apply ("the app") helps you send job application emails with pre-configured profiles and resumes.

## Data we collect

**We do not collect, transmit, or store your data on any server.**

All information stays on your device:

- Job profiles (names, email subjects, bodies)
- Resume files you choose to attach
- Application history (recruiter emails and timestamps)

## How your data is used

Data is used only to:

- Pre-fill email compose screens
- Show your saved profiles and application history inside the app

## Third-party services

When you tap **Apply**, the app opens your device's email client (Gmail on Android, Mail on iOS). Email delivery is handled by your email provider under their privacy policy.

## Data deletion

Uninstall the app or use **Clear history** in the History tab to remove application records. Profile data is removed when you delete profiles or uninstall the app.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app developer at the email listed on the Play Store or App Store listing.
